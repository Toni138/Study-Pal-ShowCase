Study Pal: Productivity & Focus Analytics Tool
Project Overview

Study Pal is a productivity application designed to help students optimize their learning habits by mitigating digital distractions and providing data-driven insights into study patterns. The core mission of the project was to solve the "constant time-checking" distraction by providing an automated, focus-oriented environment.

Key Features

Focus Timer: A specialized countdown mechanism that discourages frequent time-checking, allowing for deeper concentration sessions.


Session Recording: Automatically logs the duration of each study session categorized by subject.


Productivity Analytics: Generates insights into which subjects are receiving the most attention and identifies gaps in the user's study schedule.


Responsive UI: Designed with a mobile-first approach to ensure students can track habits across devices.

Problem-Solving & Impact
The Challenge: Students often lose focus because they constantly check their phones for the time.

The Solution: I architected a system that "takes over" the time-keeping responsibility, allowing the user to trust the app and focus entirely on the material.


Results: The app successfully tracks study frequency, helping users reallocate time to weaker subjects based on actual data rather than intuition.
